About Us

The Arellano University School of Education is the academic unit of Arellano University that is devoted to scholarship in the field of education. The school conducts review classes for Licensure Examination for Teachers.

"Our school was originally conceived as an institution dedicated to the pursuit of knowledge, no matter where the trail may lead, no matter how unpopular the result. Thus, reason and science, not tradition and dogma, have always shaped our policies, and thus we have channeled our meager efforts without deviation or break"
Florentino P. Cayco Sr.
